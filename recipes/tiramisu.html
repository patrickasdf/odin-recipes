Tiramisu

Description

Recipe for making the greatest dessert in the world, tiramisu.

Ingredients

  • 200g savoiardi biscuits
  • 1 1/4 cup coffee
  • 2tbsp Kahlua
  • 250g mascarpone
  • 3 eggs
  • 110g caster suger
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
  • cocoa

Steps

  1. Beat yolk & sugar ~10min until pale yellow/whitish and thick
  2. Add vanilla and marcarpone, beat until combined. Set aside mixture
  3. Whisk egg whites separately until stiff foams
  4. Add first half cream mixture to whites, folding through. Once combined add second half
  5. Mix coffee and Kahlua together. Quickly dip biscuits in and then place into dish, lining the bottom
  6. Spread over half the cream, then do a second layer and add other half of cream on top
  7. Cover, refrigerate for a least 4-5 hours, preferably overnight
  8. Dust with coca powder and serve
